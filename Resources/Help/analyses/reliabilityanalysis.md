### Options
#### Scale statistics
- McDonald's w
- Cronbach's alpha
  - Unstandardized
  - Standardized
- Gutmann's lambda 6
- Greatest lower bound
- Average interitem correlation
- Mean
- Standard deviation

#### Individual item statistics
- McDonald's w
- Cronbach's alpha
- Gutmann's lambda 6
- Mean
- Standard deviation
- Item-rest correlation

#### Reverse-scaled items
- Normal-scaled items
- Reverse-scaled items

#### Missing values
- Exclude cases listwise
- Exclude cases pairwise

#### Confidence interval
- Cronbach's alpha  (analytical)
  - Confidence: default is 95%
